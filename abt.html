ABOUT

provided for the educational upliftment of the students of the state.This scholarship is initiated by the Department of Backward Classes Welfare, Government of Karnataka.
The fund issued to the eligible students is directly transferred to their bank accounts.

Eligibility Details

Before applying for any of the scholarships under Vidyasiri one should note that the student satisfies all the eligibility requirements. Ineligible students should not apply as their applications will not be accepted.

The eligibility criteria for applying for each type of scholarship is given as under-

  • Eligibility Requirements for Full Course Fee concession for post matric

  • Applicant should be a permanent resident of Karnataka state.
  • Students should be enrolled in post-matric courses at schools/ institutions recognized by the Government of Karnataka.
  • They should have 75% attendance.
  • Income of parents of students belonging to SC/ST category should be upto 2 lakhs per annum.
  • Income of parents of students belonging to the Backward Classes/ PWD category should be equal or less than 1 lakh per year.


  • Eligibility Requirements for pre-matric Scholarship

  • Applicant should be domiciled in the state of Karnataka.
  • They should be enrolled in any Government or private school of the state.
  • Candidates must have secured 90% or above in SSLC/ Secondary PUC exam conducted in the month of March/April 2019.
  • This scholarship is offered once to a student for equivalent courses.
  • The annual income limit of the parent of students belonging to category 1 should be upto1 lakhs and upto 2.50 lakhs per year for categories 2A, 3A and 3B.


  • Eligibility Requirements for Undergraduate

  • Students must belong to SC/ST/OBC Category.
  • Candidate’s parental income should not exceed Rs 2,00,000 per year.
  • Students must pursue post matric course in any college in Karnataka State only.
  • Candidates are requested to submit the required documents within 7 days after the application form commences.
  • Students should have Aadhar linked bank account for Direct Benefit Transfer.

Application Process

Eligibility students who want to avail the benefit of Scholarships should submit applications. Applications can be submitted only in online mode.
Application process for 2019 has been closed now for all three types of scholarships.


Before submitting applications, it is advised to all the candidates to keep all the required documents and details ready with them. They must apply within the scheduled dates.
Late applications are accepted under any circumstances. They must keep their application no. and other details related to registration safe for further references.

Students can submit applications by following the procedure provided below-

  • Step 1- Visit the official website.
  • Step 2- On the homepage, click on the respective application links.
  • Step 3- Fill all the details carefully in the application form.
  • Step 4- Upload required documents in the space provided.
  • Step 5- Check all the details before final submission.
  • Step 6- Submit Application.
  • Step 7- Take the printout of the filled application form.
  • Step 8- After submitting the online application, send the application form along with the attested copies of each document to the concerned district Taluk Office before the due date.

Documents Required For Scholarship

Candidates must have all the relevant documents before submitting applications as they have to send the attested copy of each document to the concerned office.
The important document that each applicant is required to have for Vidaysiri scholarship are-

  • Senior Secondary Leaving Certificate (SSLC).
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) issued by the school or college.
  • Caste Certificate issued by competent authority (for SC/ST/BC/ PWD category candidates).
  • Bank Pass Book for student’s bank account details.
  • Income Certificate issued by Tahsilar or Sub-Tahsildar.
  • Residential Certificate/ Domicile certificate.
  • Latest passport-sized photograph
