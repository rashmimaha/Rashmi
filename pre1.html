Pre-Matric Scholarship

Pre-Matric Scholarship is a broad category of scholarship that offer finanacial assistance to students studying at the pre-matric level of education i.e.school education(10).
The Government of India and other state government offer a number of pre-matric scholarships for students belonging to diiferent categories including SC,ST,OBC,
General,Minority,EBC and more.the key objective of a pre-matric scholarship run by the government is to promote the school level of education among students with the help
of financial aid thereby reducin the school drop-out rate.
